## When to pick Jobscan over Vantage

- You only need keyword-matching scoring, not the full prep pack.
- You're applying through a single ATS that Jobscan has a deep optimization profile for.
- You're comfortable paying $49.95/month for a single feature.

## When to pick Vantage over Jobscan

- You want the full prep pack, not just keyword matching.
- You want company intelligence + cover letter + interview questions in one output.
- You want one-time pricing without auto-renew (£5 Starter pack).
- You want EU hosting and sole-trader operator transparency.
- You want the free 10-pack on signup with no credit card.
