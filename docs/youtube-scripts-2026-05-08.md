### Recording tips

- **Phone setup:** prop phone at face height on a stack of books, 3 ft away. Don't hold it.
- **Audio:** even cheap earphones with a mic beat phone-built-in for this length. AirPods fine.
- **Lighting:** face the window. Don't backlight yourself.
- **One take. Don't re-shoot more than 3 times.** The "indie blunt" voice gets worse with more takes — energy drops.
- **Edit:** trim dead air at start/end only. No cuts in the talking head section. Screen-record overlay can use the actual product run from earlier in the day — don't try to record + record at once.


### Recording tips for the long format

- **Three takes max.** This is a 3-minute talking head, not a Netflix doc.
- **Cut between takes** is fine — natural breaks at the section boundaries.
- **One b-roll moment only:** the GitHub commit graph at 1:00. Everything else is your face.
- **Don't write "subscribe" in the script.** Say it casually at 2:55.
