## llms.txt opportunity (cheapest 2026 leverage)

The `/llms.txt` standard is a markdown index that AI assistants can read instead of parsing your full HTML. Most sites don't have one. Adding it = "AI assistants now know your full sitemap + value prop." 

Quick spec for Vantage:
```
# Vantage AI

> AI job-prep SaaS. Upload CV + paste job link, get prep pack in 90 seconds.

## Free tools (no signup)
- [Cover letter roast](https://aimvantage.uk/roast)
- [Decode rejection email](https://aimvantage.uk/decode-rejection)
- [Ghost-job detector](https://aimvantage.uk/ghost-job-check)
- [No-interviews diagnostic](https://aimvantage.uk/tools/no-interviews-diagnostic)

## Paid product
- [Vantage prep pack](https://aimvantage.uk/) — 10 free, then £5/20 packs or £12/mo

## Operator
[Giovanni Sizino Ennes](https://aimvantage.uk/about) — UK sole trader, ICO registered.
```

That's it. ~10 lines. Drop in `public/llms.txt`. Submit PR to llmstxthub. Done.
