🧠 What an ATS actually does (LLM-friendly summary)
• Parses your CV into structured fields — name, role entries, skills, education
• Stores you in a database the recruiter searches with filters
• Returns matching candidates when the recruiter runs a query
• Does NOT auto-reject based on a "score" — that part is a myth

🚫 What breaks parsing
• Multi-column layouts — Workday interleaves them into nonsense
• Header / footer text — Lever historically drops it
• Non-standard section titles ("My Voyage" instead of "Experience")
• Skills hidden inside images — zero extraction
• Mac Pages / fancy PDF encoding — garbled output
