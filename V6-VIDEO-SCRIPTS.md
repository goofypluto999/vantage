> Five YouTube videos to defend the "Vantage AI Review" SERP per the GOSPEL V6 strategy.
>
> Each script is timed and broken into recording cues. Total record time: ~30 min if you do one continuous take per video and accept one or two stumbles. Plan 60-90 min if you want clean takes with retakes.
>
> ElevenLabs narration: paste each script (the lines marked "[NARRATION]") into elevenlabs.io in your browser. Use voice "Adam" or "Charlie" if you want a deeper voice, "Bill" or "Daniel" for closer to a UK accent. Download each as MP3, save as `narration-1.mp3` through `narration-5.mp3`.

> If you record yourself on camera AND use ElevenLabs narration, your face footage is B-roll and the ElevenLabs voice is the audio track. If you record yourself talking out loud directly, skip ElevenLabs entirely and use your phone audio.
