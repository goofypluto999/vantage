## 2. Press release draft (free distribution: PRLog, OpenPR, EIN free tier)

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Laid-off UK developer launches Vantage AI: a 90-second job-prep pack and four free tools for the AI-redundancy generation**

*London, United Kingdom — 10 May 2026* — Vantage AI (https://aimvantage.uk), an indie British SaaS built by sole-trader Giovanni Sizino Ennes after his own redundancy in November 2025, has opened to the public. The product turns a CV and a job advert into a complete interview-prep pack — company brief, tailored cover letter in four tones, mock interview questions, fit score and personal pitch — in roughly 90 seconds.

Every new account gets ten prep packs free with no card required. Paid tiers begin at £5 (Starter), £12 (Pro) and £20 (Premium) per month. Four additional tools are completely free, with no signup: a CV roast, a rejection-email decoder, a ghost-job checker, and a "why am I getting no interviews" diagnostic. An open-source companion, CV Mirror, runs an ATS-style scan entirely in the user's browser and is available on GitHub at github.com/goofypluto999/cv-mirror-mcp.

"I lost my job in November. I built the tool I wished I'd had during those three months of applications that went nowhere," said Ennes. "The free tier and the four no-signup tools aren't a marketing trick — they're the version of the product I needed when I had no money and 200 applications to send."

Vantage AI is operated by Giovanni Sizino Ennes as a UK sole trader, with ICO data-protection registration in progress. The product is being built in public, with a public changelog and revenue figures shared on the company's X account.

The launch arrives during a year in which AI-driven layoffs accounted for an estimated 26 percent of all April 2026 redundancies, according to Challenger, Gray & Christmas. Vantage AI is positioned at the opposite side of that trend: software for the people the layoffs are aimed at.

**About Vantage AI**
Vantage AI is a one-person UK SaaS that helps jobseekers prepare for interviews using AI. Founded in 2026 by Giovanni Sizino Ennes after his own redundancy. Headquartered in the United Kingdom. https://aimvantage.uk.
